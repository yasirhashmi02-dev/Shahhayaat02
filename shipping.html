Shipping Policy

Delivery Charges

  • Free delivery on all orders above ₹499
  • Flat ₹60 shipping on orders below ₹499
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) available across India — additional ₹30 COD handling fee applies

Delivery Timeframes

  • Metro cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, Kolkata): 3–5 working days
  • Other cities and towns: 5–7 working days
  • Remote/hilly areas: 7–10 working days

Order Processing

  • Orders placed before 2:00 PM IST are dispatched the same day (Monday–Saturday)
  • Orders placed after 2:00 PM or on Sundays/public holidays are dispatched the next working day
  • You will receive a WhatsApp message with your tracking number once dispatched

📱 WhatsApp Tracking: Once your order ships, we will send you the courier name and tracking number directly on WhatsApp. You can also message us at +91 70510 56287 to check your order status anytime.

Returns & Refund Policy

We stand behind every product we make. If you are not satisfied, we want to make it right.

What Can Be Returned

  • Products returned within 7 days of delivery are accepted
  • Item must be unused, sealed in original packaging
  • Damaged or defective products — we will replace or refund fully, no questions asked
  • Wrong product delivered — full replacement at no cost to you

What Cannot Be Returned

  • ✕Opened or partially used products (unless damaged/defective)
  • ✕Products returned after 7 days of delivery
  • ✕Products without original packaging

How to Initiate a Return

  • WhatsApp us at +91 70510 56287 within 7 days of receiving your order
  • Send your order details and a photo of the product
  • We will arrange pickup or guide you on how to send it back

Refund Timeline

  • Refunds are processed within 5–7 working days of receiving the returned product
  • Online payments: refunded to original payment source
  • COD orders: refunded via bank transfer (NEFT) — please share your bank details via WhatsApp
📦

Packaging & Quality Assurance

  • All products are sealed at our WHO-GMP certified facility before dispatch
  • Tamper-evident packaging — do not accept if seal is broken on delivery
  • Products are packed with bubble wrap and cushioning to prevent damage in transit
  • Temperature-sensitive products are packed with appropriate thermal protection

If your package arrives damaged, take a photo immediately before opening and send it to us on WhatsApp. We will replace the order at no cost.

  • For WhatsApp/direct orders: use the process described above
  • For any issues with Amazon orders, you can also contact us on WhatsApp and we will help resolve it
