Common Questions About PanaSip

Chemical antacids (Digene, Gelusil) only neutralise existing acid temporarily. PanaSip heals the stomach lining (Mulethi's anti-ulcer action), reduces acid secretion (Shatavari) and strengthens the gastric mucosa — addressing the cause, not just the symptom.

Many users successfully reduce or stop PPI dependency with PanaSip over 6–8 weeks combined with dietary changes. Do not stop PPIs abruptly — taper with your doctor's guidance as rebound hyperacidity is common.
