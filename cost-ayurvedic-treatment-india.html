What Affects Ayurvedic Product Pricing?

  • Certification level: WHO-GMP and ISO 9001:2015 certified products cost more than uncertified ones — but guarantee quality, purity and accurate dosing
  • Herb rarity: Kutki (Picrorhiza kurroa) and Jatamansi are high-altitude Himalayan herbs with limited availability — products containing them carry a small premium
  • Formulation type: Classical bhasmas (Loha Bhasma, Shankha Bhasma) require intensive traditional processing — priced higher than simple herbal powders
  • Multi-herb combinations: A formula combining 4–5 therapeutic herbs at proper doses will cost more than a single-herb product — but delivers far better results
  • Brand vs. generic: Certified brands like Shah Hayaat carry quality assurance that generic market products cannot guarantee
